## Part 2: Challenge Summary

**Overall risk assessment**: **LOW**

While the components are solid, compile cleanly, and meet all requirements, the adversarial review identified two minor styling and transition vulnerabilities that could affect user experience under specific conditions (mobile viewports and animation smoothness).

## Challenges

### [Low] Challenge 1: FAQDerecho Accordion Text Clipping on Small Mobile Screen
- **Assumption challenged**: That the answer text in the FAQ items will always fit within the fixed height restriction of `max-h-48` (`192px`).
- **Attack scenario**: On a small mobile device (e.g. 320px screen width), layout paddings reduce the content width. The answer text of FAQ 2 (212 characters) will wrap to 6-7 lines. The height of 7 lines of text plus margins exceeds `192px`. Because the accordion container has `overflow-hidden` applied, the bottom part of the text will be clipped and rendered invisible.
- **Blast radius**: Truncated FAQ answers on small viewports.
- **Mitigation**: Adjust `max-h-48` to `max-h-96` or `max-h-[500px]` in `FAQDerecho.jsx` line 69.

### [Low] Challenge 2: SyllabusDerecho Accordion Transition Overflow Glitch
- **Assumption challenged**: That CSS transitions on `max-height` render smoothly when `overflow-hidden` is applied conditionally rather than continuously.
- **Attack scenario**: In `SyllabusDerecho.jsx`, the `overflow-hidden` class is conditionally applied ONLY when `isOpen` is false (line 120: `className={\`transition-all duration-500 ease-in-out \${isOpen ? 'max-h-[800px] opacity-100' : 'max-h-0 opacity-0 overflow-hidden'}\`}`). During the opening transition (`isOpen = true`), the container does not have `overflow-hidden`. As a result, the list of topics immediately pops out to its full height during the transition, rendering over the next modules before the container height catches up, causing a visible jump.
- **Blast radius**: Jarring visual transition glitches when opening syllabus modules.
- **Mitigation**: Apply `overflow-hidden` unconditionally to the accordion container so it is active during both opening and closing transitions:
  ```jsx
  className={`transition-all duration-500 ease-in-out overflow-hidden ${isOpen ? 'max-h-[800px] opacity-100' : 'max-h-0 opacity-0'}`}
  ```
